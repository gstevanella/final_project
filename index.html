Earthquakes Map

This dataset is a geocoded extension of a selection of disasters from the Centre for Research on the Epidemiology of Disastersâ€™ (CRED) emergency events database (EM-DAT) (Guha-Sapir, Below, and Hoyois, 2014). The dataset encompasses 39,953 locations for 9,924 disastersthat occurred worldwide in the years 1960 to 2018. All floods, storms (typhoons, monsoons etc.), earthquakes, landslides (wet and dry), droughts, volcanic activity and extreme temperatures that were recorded in EM-DAT during these 58 years are included in the dataset.1
